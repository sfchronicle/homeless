How many people live on our streets?

By Joaquin Palomino

June 28, 2016

Grimes is a former famous Fisherman's Wharf act. He used to be known as the Human Jukebox. But times got tough and he lived for a time in a homeless encampment off Evans Street in San Francisco. He was surrounded by discarded clothes. The encampment has since been cleaned up and Grimes now spends his time as a recycler near the Mission district. Photo: Brant Ward, The Chronicle
