Beyond Homelessness

{% include '_components/_social_links.html' %}

The Chronicle has joined with more than 70 other news organizations to focus attention on the seemingly intractable problem of homelessness in the Bay Area. As part of the SF Homeless Project, The Chronicle's Beyond Homelessness series explores possible solutions that might ease, if not end, the suffering of thousands of people living on our streets, and improve the quality of life for all residents. To date, the SF Homeless Project has been emulated in more than a dozen U.S. cities where homelessness remains a humanitarian crisis.
